About

Assata Community Park was established in 2025 in honor of Assata Bamba who passed away as an infant due to a congenital heart defect. Assata did not get the chance to play in a community park and so, her parents built this park for children to enjoy and live carefree lives as children should. The park has a basketball court, a swimming pool, a playground, and some hiking trails. The park is maintained through donations. The only rule of this park is that we ask for you to keep it clean and safe for our children.

This website serves to connect residents and visitor to the park's information, resources, and services in real time. Information about the park, its hours, location, emergency-related closures, and survey will be available on this website. The goal is to make information about the park concise and easily accessible to users!

SWIMMING POOL

The park pool serves as a vital recreational area for swimming, relaxation, and socializing. This facility is commonly found in the parks, providing a refreshing spot for individuals and families to cool off, work out, and engage in water activities, particularly during the warmer months. Please remember that adult supervision is essential at all times to ensure everyone's safety.

HIKING TRAIL

The hiking trail in the park is an unpaved path specifically intended for walking or hiking across natural landscapes. To make the most of your outdoor experience, take note that these trails can differ significantly in length, difficulty, and various features. As you explore, seize the chance to engage in physical activity, appreciate the beauty of nature, and relieve stress.

NATURE

To fully appreciate the park sunset, take a moment to abserve and engage with this beautiful natural spectacle. Notice how the sky transforms into a canvas of vibrant colors, signaling the peaceful end of the day. Allow these moments to instill a sense of serenity and remind you of the beauty that nature offers. Embrace the calming atmosphere, and let it enhance your connection to the environment.

PLAYGROUND

The park playground is a designated outdoor space where children can engage in play. This area typically includes various features such a swings, slides, and climbing structures. The playgrounds are important for promoting physical activity and encouraging social interactions among children. Remember to supervise children while they play to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.

BASKETBALL COURT

The park basketball court is an area designated for playing basketball , it is located outdoors and marked according to standard dimensions. To ensure durability and resilience against weather conditions and high usage, it is typically made from robust materials such as concrete or asphalt. The court provide a venue for community memebers to engage in physical activity, foster social connections, and enjoy friendly competition.

PICNIC TABLE

The picnic in the park is a charming outdoor dining experience, typically set against picturesque backdrops. It allows for unwinding, mingling, and appreciating the great outdoors. This occasion offers a perfect break from the daily grind, letting you savor good food, engaging conversations and enjoyable activities amid nature's beauty.
